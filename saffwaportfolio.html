About Me

I’m Saffwa, a media student at Bahria University, exploring storytelling, production, and everything in between. My background is rooted in Kashmir and that shows up in my work, my visuals, and the way I think about narratives.

Filmmaking and storytelling aren’t just hobbies to me, they’re tools to reflect what we see, what we hide, and what we hope for. I notice the small things, how a frame feels, when a moment shifts, what’s unsaid in a scene. I enjoy the quiet roles: scripting, organizing, observing. That’s why I’m drawn to script supervision, camera work, and anchoring and they bring structure to emotion. I’m still learning, but I learn fast. Whether it’s writing a draft script or quietly observing on a film set, I take it seriously. I value precision, responsibility, and the energy behind the camera and I’m here to grow by doing.
